4.0 out of 5 starsExcellent product overall! Some room for improvement.
September 15, 2017
Size: MediumColor: #1Verified Purchase
I installed these chair covers over my old vinyl-wrapped faux-leather chairs and they look amazing! - Better than I originally expected.
The fit is mostly perfect (following the dimensions off the product description). I would not hesitate to buy more of these. Shipping is relatively quick too considering they are getting shipped straight out of China.

However there are a couple of things that I feel are lacking (which I would not mind paying a premium for to get addressed):
- The fabric is very slippery. It's especially an issue if you're putting them on top of a slippery material (e.g. vinyl). Would be nice to add a rubber coating to the underside to prevent slip when sitting on them.
- The portion of the cover which sits above the back-side of the seat "floats" somewhat above the chair itself. I had to stretch out fishing line to get it to sit nicely, after which point it actually looks like part of the chair itself. I'd like to see some string directly woven into the fabric that I'd be able to use to tie the back-side down instead of having to improvise my own solution.
