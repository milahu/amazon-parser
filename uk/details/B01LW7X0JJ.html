Product description

Size:Medium  |  Colour:White + Black

Refresh your dining room in style with this Stretch Dining Room Chair Cover
1.Fall decor chair protectors comes in a colorful pallet that blends with most room decor, and it will add a stylish appearance to your dining room area.
2.Made from a blend of polyester and spandex fabric, universal chair cover gives you long-lasting durability and easy maintenance.
3.Spandex chair slipcovers also features an elastic closure to give a snug fit on the chair that also gives a neat look to the furniture.
4.Easy to keep clean, kitchen chair covers are resistant to staining, and the easy care fabric can be safely washed in the washer when needed.

Specification
Material: Elastic Cloth
Main Ingredients: Polyurethane Elastic Fiber Fabric(Spandex)
Package include: 1 x chair cover

Suitable for Medium or Large Size Chair(1 inch = 2.54 cm):
Medium Size:
Back Height: 16.5 Inch-21.3 Inch (42-54cm)
Back Width: 15 Inch-17.7 Inch (38-45cm)
Seat Length: 15 Inch-17.7 Inch (38-45cm)
Seat Width: 15 Inch-17.7 Inch (38-45cm)
Seat Thickness: 1.5 Inch-4 Inch (4-10cm)

Large Size:
Back Height: 21.7 Inch-26.4 Inch (55-67cm)
Back Width: 15 Inch-17.7 Inch (38-45cm)
Seat Length: 15 Inch-17.7 Inch (38-45cm)
Seat Width: 15 Inch-17.7 Inch (38-45cm)
Seat Thickness: 1.5 Inch-4 Inch (4-10cm)
